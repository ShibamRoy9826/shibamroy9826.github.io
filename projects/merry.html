Commands Or Examples
(Its not mandatory to write in the same way.. these are only examples)

  1. Browser -> Opens your default browser
  2. Take a screenshot -> Takes a screenshot
  3. Clean my pc -> Cleans The junk files of the computer to make it run fast(especially for low-end computers)
  4. Camera -> Opens your Camera
  5. Type the name of any famous website like Facebook, Gmail,etc. -> Opens that particular website
  6. Wiki or wikipedia [Your topic name] -> Searches about your topic in wikipedia
  7. Sound control (Increase sound, decrease sound, medium sound) -> Alters the sound of your computer
  8. max volume or full volume -> sets to max system sound
  9. close [Your Application] -> Closes the particular application
  10. [A] + [B] -> Adds the given numbers
  11. [A] - [B] -> Subtracts the given numbers
  12. [A] * [B] -> Multiplies the given numbers
  13. [A] / [B] -> Divides the given numbers
  14. [A] + [B] -> Adds the given numbers
  15. Play music -> Plays a music randomly which is available in your computer
  16. Pause/ Unpause Music -> Pause or unpause the music
  17. Stop music -> Stops the music if it's playing.
  18. Close or Bye -> Shuts down itself
  19. Shut down/ restart/ log off -> Does the specific action to your computer
  20. Tell me a joke -> Tells you a funny joke
  21. Take a photo -> Clicks a photo with your camera
  22. Voice typing -> toggles voice typing mode
  23. add music [Any Youtube URL] -> Adds any music from a youtube url
  24. remove music [Music name] -> Removes the music which you dont want anymore
  25. Introduce yourself -> Talks about itself
  26. Open [application name] -> Opens any application you want
